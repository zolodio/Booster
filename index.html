How to Use

Getting Started

Track your Avatar Quick Strike TCG collection by marking cards you own.

Adding Cards

Click any card to open the detail view. Use the quantity controls to set how many copies you own, then add to your collection.

Card Zoom

Click the card image in the detail view to open a fullscreen zoom. Click anywhere outside the image or the X button to close it.

Market Prices

Cards with eBay sold data show a price widget in the detail view — low, average, and high from recent sales. Prices update daily automatically.

Search & Filter

Use the search box to find cards by name or number. Click the X in the search bar to clear your search. Filter by card type, rarity, and ownership status.

Sorting

Choose how to sort your cards (number, name, rarity, type, quantity, or price). Click the arrow button to reverse the sort order.

Collection Progress

The stats bar shows how many of the 248 core cards you own. Your total card count includes bonus cards from special products.

Export & Import

Export your collection as CSV or save code. Import a previously exported save code or CSV file to restore your collection.

Compare Collections

Paste a friend's save code to compare. Cards they own will be highlighted with a blue badge showing their quantity.

Save Codes

Your save code is automatically generated and displayed at the bottom. Select it to copy and save offline.

Pack Calculators

Find them under Portfolio. Each rarity tab (Zenemental, Chamber, Rare, Uncommon, Common) uses the Coupon Collector formula to estimate how many more packs you need to complete that subset of core cards, based on how many you already own.

Friends, Chat & Trades

Sign in under Profile, set a username, then access Friends, Chat, Trades and Forum.
